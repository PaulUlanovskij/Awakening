I put the pasta in the microwave oven and yawned.
She sat on the table and watched the dish rotate with attention. Her tail swung in a large arc, stopping for a moment before changing direction. She slid her legs off the table and waved them in the air.
I was fighting the sleep and looking at her.
The oven rang. She flopped her ears and smiled.
I took the dish out and put it before her. She took the utensils, put the dish on her lap, still sitting on the table, and started eating with joy.
